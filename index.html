About Me:


I am a detail oriented, solutions-minded professional transitioning from a career in sales into software development. Having recently graduated from Indiana University, I began a career in sales and advertising and sold my first custom advertising campaign two days after completing my training. Despite having a knack for sales, I felt that I was missing creative engagement in my career.

I've always had an affinity with technology and the way it changes lives. When I discovered 1150 through a friend, I knew I found the home for my professional renaissance. Being surrounded by continuous learning, innovative programs, and new technologies has opened my eyes to the power of such an exciting field and the freedom it yields to those who pursue it. Thus my pursuit began.

My dream as a developer is to create apps that are original to the world, limitless in potential, yet simplistic to the user.
